THE REAL
THING

Bangkok Thai isn't dressed up. No mood lighting, no padded booth — just a counter, a wok, and cooks who actually know what they're doing. The kind of place where the food is the whole point.

Most Thai places in America quietly dial back the spice. We don't. Order a 4 and you'll feel it. Order a 6 and you'll remember it. The flavors are real: aromatic, fermented, layered, and genuinely hot.

Students have been coming here for decades. Alumni drive back for it during homecoming. One reviewer wrote she dreams about it when she leaves Champaign. That's not marketing — that's just what happens when the food is actually good.

"Best restaurant on Green St. Period."
— Claire Qin, UIUC student
