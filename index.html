Our Promise

Real Heat.
Real Thai.

Unlike most Thai restaurants in the US, we don't tone down the spice. Our dishes reflect genuine Thai flavor profiles — aromatic, bold, and made fresh to order. Choose your heat level and we'll deliver exactly what you asked for.

From day one, Bangkok Thai has been a campus staple. Students, professors, alumni coming back for homecoming — they all return for the same thing: food that actually tastes like Thailand.
